Restaurant Rules & Policies

Timing & Reservations

  • Tables are held for 15 minutes after reservation time
  • Maximum dining time is 2 hours during peak hours
  • Last order taken 30 minutes before closing

Dining Etiquette

  • Proper attire required (no swimwear or sleepwear)
  • Please silence your mobile phones
  • Children must be supervised at all times

Prohibited Items

  • No outside food or beverages
  • No smoking or vaping inside premises
  • No pets allowed (service animals excepted)

Payment Policy

  • Cash, credit/debit cards, and UPI accepted
  • 18% gratuity added for parties of 6 or more
  • No refunds on already served items
